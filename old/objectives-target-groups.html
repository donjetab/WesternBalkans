Objectives & Target Groups

The formal project objectives and the main groups the project is designed to support.

Main target groups

Social work students Future professionals studying at partner institutions.
Current social workers Practitioners who need specialized knowledge and skills related to migration.
Educators Staff involved in social sciences and psychology programs who will revise curricula and develop courses.

Project scope

The project aims to improve the competencies of social workers and students in Kosovo and Albania so they can provide better support to migrant populations.

Objectives

O1

Guidelines for curriculum integration

Create guidelines for integrating migration-related topics into existing curricula.

O2

Curriculum revision

Revise and enrich curricula of social sciences and psychology faculties to incorporate migration topics.

O3

Digital short micro-credentials

Develop and pilot digital short micro-credential courses tailored for practicing social workers.

O4

Capacity building for educators

Provide initiatives for educators and trainers involved in curriculum enhancement and micro-credential course design.
