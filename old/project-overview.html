Project Overview

The full project story, rewritten into a clearer page while keeping the actual site content.

What the project addresses

The WB-Edu4Migration project addresses a critical skills gap among social workers in Kosovo and Albania regarding migrant populations. Educational institutions have not yet fully integrated migration-related coursework into social work programs. The project responds by reforming curricula, developing digital micro-credential courses, and implementing capacity-building activities.

Main purpose

The goal is to create a more inclusive and supportive environment for migrants while helping social workers better advocate for their needs. The project combines higher education reform with flexible online learning opportunities for current practitioners.

Homepage message

The live homepage emphasizes equipping social workers to transform challenges into opportunities for migrants, and empowering both future and current professionals through curriculum enhancement and continuous development.
